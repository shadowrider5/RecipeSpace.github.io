Recipe Details


Cook 1 hr
Prep 5 mins
Servings 4
Ingredients:
Large Sweet Potatoes 6
White Sugar 2 Cup(s)
Ground Cinammon 1 Tablespoon

preparation steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a sheet pan with aluminum foil.
  2. Poke skin of potatoes 4 to 6 times with a fork. Place on the prepared pan.
  3. Roast until fork-tender, about 1 hour.
